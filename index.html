Moody Wallet

The "Moody Wallet," designed by NovaTech Corporation, is a smart wallet concept featuring an LED lights with face for displaying "moods" or statuses, a fund indicator for balance tracking, and a magnetic charger. It includes a battery health indicator and aims to integrate technology with traditional wallet functionality. The sketch highlights its focus on user interaction and convenience.

About

Enhance user interaction with a mood-based LED display, simplify fund management with a balance indicator, and improve convenience with wireless magnetic charging. Ensure clear battery health status, merge traditional wallet functions with modern tech, and create a unique, personalized accessory.

Wireless Charger

A wireless charger combines convenience an d functionality, letting users charge their "Moody Wallet" on the go while storing cards and cash. It is a smart, modern accessory for everyday use.
